Our Story

Founded in 2025

Our journey began with a simple love for delicious food and a dream to serve happiness in every bite. What started as a small idea soon turned into a passion for creating fresh, flavorful momos using authentic recipes and quality ingredients. Inspired by street-style flavors, we focus on delivering not just taste, but an unforgettable experience. Every momo we serve is crafted with care, bringing people together and creating moments filled with joy, warmth, and satisfaction.
